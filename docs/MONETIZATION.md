## VC Pitch Framework

### One-Liner
> *"We're building the autonomous nervous system for cities."*

### Problem (30 seconds)
> India has 100 Smart Cities with ₹48,000Cr budget. They've built dashboards. None of them coordinate. Traffic, power, emergency, flood — all running blind. 2,400 people die annually from cascading infrastructure failures. A flood causes traffic, traffic blocks ambulances, power goes down, signals go dark, people die. The problem isn't sensors — cities have plenty. The problem is coordination.

### Solution (30 seconds)
> NEXUS-GOV deploys AI agents for each city domain. These agents share a unified knowledge graph and coordinate autonomously. When a flood hits, the traffic agent clears corridors before ambulances dispatch. The power agent reroutes before substations fail. All in under 30 seconds. With full audit trail and human override.

### Market (15 seconds)
> Smart city market: $6.8T globally by 2030. India alone: 100 cities, ₹48K Cr allocated, <2% on AI coordination. We're the first product that makes cities act, not just observe.

### Traction (15 seconds)
> Working prototype built in 24 hours at NeuraX 2.0 hackathon. 4 domain agents coordinating. 3 crisis scenarios. Cinematic command center. Won [Best AI Project / Grand Prize]. Ready for pilot deployment.

### Ask
> Raising ₹2Cr seed. 18-month runway. Target: 3 city pilots, 1 state partnership, team of 8.
